Leadership perspective and execution philosophy behind The Pundit Place.

Jude Onwugbaramuko

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Jude Onwugbaramuko is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of The Pundit Place. He is a Certified Business Analysis Professional (CBAP®) accredited by the International Institute of Business Analysis™ (IIBA®), and a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Management Consultants (FCIMC), Canada/USA.

He is also the Co-founder of Juann-Harbor Ventures, where he supports founders, boards, and executive teams in navigating complex business transformation, strategy execution, and operating-model design.

His advisory and leadership experience spans engagements across Nigeria, the United States, the Middle East, and North Africa, with a focus on strengthening governance, improving execution discipline, and aligning organisational structures with strategic intent.

Jude’s consulting philosophy is execution-led and outcomes-driven. He is recognised for translating vision into structured, measurable results through disciplined business analysis, strong people systems, and practical leadership frameworks.

Professional interests include: Business analysis, management consulting, healthcare systems, executive advisory, human capital strategy, venture partnerships, emerging markets, and technology-enabled business models.
