Official Programme Transcript

This page serves as an official reference for the structure and scope of The Pundit Place Business Analyst Mentorship Programme.

Purpose of This Page

The programme transcript is provided for verification and reference purposes. It outlines the topics and learning areas covered during the mentorship programme.

Employers, institutions, and third parties may use this page to understand the scope of the programme completed by participants.

Programme Structure Overview

  • Introduction to the Business Analyst role in organisations
  • Understanding business problems and objectives
  • Requirements elicitation and documentation
  • Process mapping and analysis
  • Stakeholder identification and engagement
  • Communication and facilitation techniques
  • Supporting decision-making and execution
  • Sector-tailored case discussions

Delivery Format

  • Structured weekly mentorship sessions
  • Practical exercises and guided discussions
  • Mentor-led feedback and review
  • Application of concepts to real-world scenarios

Verification

Completion of the mentorship programme is subject to participation and engagement criteria defined by The Pundit Place.
