Working at The Pundit Place

The Pundit Place is a focused advisory firm. We work closely with founders and leadership teams on complex growth, execution, and organisational challenges.

Our work is not volume-driven consulting. We value clarity of thought, execution discipline, and practical problem-solving.

Who We Look For

  • Professionals with strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • People who value execution as much as strategy
  • Individuals comfortable working across ambiguity and complexity
  • Those interested in organisational design, leadership, and people systems
  • Strong communicators who can work closely with senior stakeholders

Open Roles

We do not maintain a large number of open roles at all times. Opportunities arise based on client needs and project pipelines.

Typical roles may include:

  • Business Analysts
  • Advisory Associates
  • Research & Insight Contributors
  • Project-Based Specialists

How to Apply

If you are interested in working with The Pundit Place, please reach out with a brief introduction and relevant experience.

We review applications on a rolling basis and respond when there is a potential fit.
