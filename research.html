Research

Our research focuses on the recurring challenges organisations face as they move from idea to execution and from early growth to scale.

Our Research Focus

The research at The Pundit Place is grounded in real advisory work with founders, leadership teams, and growing organisations.

Rather than abstract theory, we study patterns that repeatedly emerge across ventures, industries, and organisational stages — particularly where growth breaks down.

Key Areas of Research

  • Venture creation and early-stage execution challenges
  • Organisational design during growth and scale
  • Leadership clarity, accountability, and decision-making
  • People systems as infrastructure for performance
  • Governance and operating discipline in scaling organisations

How We Use Research

Research directly informs our advisory engagements. Insights are used to:

  • Anticipate structural and leadership challenges before they emerge
  • Design operating models appropriate to organisational maturity
  • Strengthen execution frameworks and people systems
  • Support evidence-informed decision-making

This ensures that our work remains practical, relevant, and grounded in observed reality.

From Research to Insight

Selected research themes are shared publicly through our insights and LinkedIn newsletter, where we explore implications for founders and leadership teams.
